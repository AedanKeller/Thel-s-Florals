Welcome to Thel's Floral

At Thel's Floral, we believe that flowers have the power to transform moments into memories. Since 2010, our passionate team of floral artists has been bringing joy, comfort, and beauty to our community through thoughtfully crafted floral arrangements.

Located in the heart of the city, our boutique florist shop sources the freshest, seasonal blooms from local growers and international markets to ensure the highest quality and most unique selection for our customers.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, expressing sympathy, or simply brightening someone's day, we're here to help you find the perfect floral expression.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of floral services, tailored to meet your unique needs and preferences:

Custom Bouquets

Hand-crafted arrangements for any occasion, from birthday celebrations to anniversary surprises, or just because.

Wedding Flowers

Stunning bridal bouquets, centerpieces, and venue decorations that bring your wedding vision to life.

Event Florals

Transform your corporate events, parties, and special celebrations with elegant floral installations.

Indoor Plants

Beautiful selection of houseplants, succulents, and terrariums to bring natural beauty into your home or office.

Floral Workshops

Learn the art of floral design through our hands-on workshops for individuals, groups, and special events.

Flower Subscriptions

Regular deliveries of seasonal blooms to your home or office, bringing continuous joy throughout the year.
